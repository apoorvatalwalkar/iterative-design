Context

About the Project

Warp is a startup trying to modernize the conventional terminal with artificial intelligence features. With features like automatic command completion, smart suggestions for tons of CLI tools, and numerous quality-of-life improvements for users, Warp aims to optimize every developer’s workflow. Our task is to design a community page for Warp, where users can share everything from simple workflows to complex automations, even if they’re an individual hobbyist dev or an industry professional who relies on Warp every day. This community page will be a website supported on both desktop and mobile devices.

Getting Started

Present Factors

Existing Considerations

Users currently share solutions through fragmented channels (GitHub, Reddit, Discord, Twitter). Some solutions are shared via blog posts but lack standardization and easy implementation. Competitive products may have partial solutions but lack comprehensive community features. Interface needs to balance discovery with ease of implementation

Customer Motivations

Desire to improve productivity and output quality when working with AI tools. Want to learn best practices from other experienced users. Seek to stay current with rapidly evolving AI techniques and approaches. Hope to reduce time spent creating rules, plans, and prompts from scratch. Interested in benchmarking their own approaches against others in the community. Motivated to contribute their knowledge to help others and establish professional credibility

Problems with Existing Solutions

Significant time spent reinventing solutions that others have already created. Difficulty finding high-quality, relevant examples of rules, plans, and prompts. Uncertainty about whether their current approach is optimal or efficient. Limited visibility into how others are solving similar problems. No streamlined way to implement discovered solutions into their existing projects. Frustration with the trial-and-error process when creating new prompts or rules. Lack of feedback on their own created content

Competitors

Windsurf, Cursor, Claude Code, Devin

Goals of the Project

Design an communit hub for users where they can post and view rules, plans, and prompt templates.

Specifics

  • Create a template for users to standardize posts and streamline the posting process
  • Increase access to guides so users can learn from other experienced users

Part Two

Sketching and Wireframing

Problem Statement

Warp developers often find AI-powered solutions to their development woes that can be heavily tailored to individual needs. Such information in the forms of Rules, Prompts, and Plans (RPP) is scattered across the internet (in forum posts, blogs, social media, and more) and easily becomes outdated. Warp Community aims to centralize and maintain this knowledge, by providing an easy way for users to post, organize, and search for it. This should help developers from various backgrounds end up on the same page and enhance their usage of Warp.

Target Users

AI development professionals who are actively working with AI tools to build or integrate solutions. They span from intermediate developers who are looking to improve their workflow efficiency to advanced power users who create complex automations. They typically work in small to medium-sized teams, are technology early adopters, and value both practical solutions and innovation. They regularly use AI devtools but feel they're not maximizing their potential.
